Sourcing, roasting and serving coffees that we love.

The Roaster


All of our coffees are roasted on site, right in the back of our Pioneer Square café. Amid jute bags of green coffee from the best small-lot farms in the world, you'll find us roasting, cupping and bagging our favorites of the season.



The Cafe


Awash in light on even the greyest days, our café is the perfect place to settle in with a cup of coffee. Here you'll find expertly crafted es presso and batch brews, teas from Song and Harbor Herbalist, and pastries from our neighbors at The London Plane. Stop by and see what's good.
